  • Customer Relations: This is a unique feature which enables Creators/Product Owners to manage all consumer information/concerns directly on the dashboard while exporting the details for marketing purposes.
  • Explore creators: This feature is one which would enable other Creators and products to be found on the platform using and extensive search option and explore page.
  • Digital Wallet: This is a very convenient way of storing money digitally on the platform for easy access while purchasing merchandise.
  • Content Library: This Library feature enables Users to access their products at anytime by creating an account and logging in to their profile.
  • Stories: This is an interesting feature specially designed to engage Users of Boat Island. It allows the Creators/Product Owners to post on their profile, stories of their work-in-progress or a new merchandise daily.
