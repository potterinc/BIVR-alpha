Since Data Collection is a compulsory part of BOAT ISLAND website/app, you must be aware that certain data can be collected and processed while you are using our website. Such approved methods of data collection are as follows:

  1. Registration and Completion of Purchase/Payment: This comes to play when Users formally indicate their interest to use BOAT ISLAND website/App by filling out the provided form with their Identity details, Contact details, Bank details and all other details mentioned above.

2. The Use of Cookies

For quality assurance purposes on BOAT ISLAND website, its Management will from time to time gather information about Users‘ computer in a bid to provide appropriate services or products to the Users. However, it is important to note that data gathered in this light is solely “statistical” which may be shared with advertisers. This means that other provided personal/ identifyinginformation and that of your activities on our website shall be kept confidential and shall not be shared to these advertisers.

Usually, cookies are used to collect data about general online usage by through a cookie file which could be downloaded without prompting. Whenever this happens, it will be placed on your device‘s hard drive for the sole purpose of data collection. As earlier stated, a cookie remains a tool that would enable us to improve the services/ products we offer you.

However, every device has the option to either accept or decline cookies through the “cookie button”. The web browser options include an "enable" button to enable cookies. It is important to note that declining cookies could limit users access to some services on BOAT ISLAND website. Also note that we do not have control over our advertisers‘ cookies if ever used.

Most importantly, cookies are used to store Users‘ login details and remember all Users‘ settings.
