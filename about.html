The Boat Island Alpha (BIVR-α)

An Interactive VR Sandbox

BIVR is a Multiplayer Virtual Reality (VR) sandbox. It is a for boats (Content creators) and our Islanders (Consumers) which is a virtual space where islanders can visit, connect with each other and enjoy exclusive contents and entertainment from different boats around the world. This Meta-verse interactive sandbox, is accesed with a token in order to gain access. Access to the boat island is exclusively FREE for our Islanders, to become and islander, simply register on The Boat Island Portal. BIVR-α is available of Android and VR console
